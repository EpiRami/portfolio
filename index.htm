About the course

Generative AI for Work & Research — 8‑Week Live Cohort

A hands‑on bootcamp by GRAPH Courses (University of Geneva) that blends academic rigor with practical AI skills. Emphasis on no‑code tools, reproducible workflows, and a capstone project.

Bootcamp features

Learning experience

  • Weekly live workshops & help sessions
  • Private community & personalized feedback
  • Capstone coaching & lifetime tutorial library
  • Shareable certificate
Assessment

Grading rubric

  • Online quizzes — 15%
  • Workshop assignments — 35%
  • Attendance & participation — 10%
  • Final project — 40%
  • Passing: 80% • Attend ≥5 workshops
Why this course

Applied & affordable

Non‑profit program with a supportive ecosystem—mentors, peer feedback, and real‑world AI projects grounded in research practice.

Modules overview

Module 1

Websites, documents & presentations

How LLMs work; generating Markdown/LaTeX/HTML; building simple research websites; structured docs; AI‑assisted scientific slides/posters.

Module 2

AI‑automated data analysis

Rapid analysis with Code Interpreter; automated dashboards; dataset transformation; batch file processing; automated reports & viz; spreadsheet automations.

Module 3

GenAI for research & text analysis

Efficient literature reviews (Elicit/LiteRev); text classification; sentiment & trend analysis; knowledge maps; summarization pipelines; synthesis systems.

Module 4

Custom GPTs & chatbots

Context windows, RAG; custom GPTs; Botpress chatbots; integrating knowledge bases into research workflows.

Module 5

AI for task automation

Agent‑based systems; email response automations; browser control agents; orchestration of complex flows.

Module 6

GenAI for communication & outreach

AI‑generated summaries; scientific figures & diagrams; video abstracts; visualization prompts; content scheduling.

Module 7

Capstone & showcase

Project checkpoints, peer feedback, final presentations, and public Q&A.
